Requirements

Build a creative app that uses Google’s Generative AI tools with Gemini!

Provide a URL to your code repository for judging and testing. Include a video (about 3 minutes) that demonstrates your submission. Videos must be uploaded to YouTube, Vimeo, or Facebook Video and made public. Google Cloud Project ID. Identify which Gemini API you are using (Google AI or Vertex AI). Low-Code Prize. The Most Impressive Low-Code submission prize will be judged based on the efficiency, creativity, and accessibility of the creation process. Video submissions of workflows showing uses of low-code tools should aim to demonstrate how such tools can speed up workflows, improve the uniqueness or creativity of a project, expand the ability of individuals with limited LLM development experience to build with generative AI, or otherwise demonstrate benefits of using low-code tools in development workflows. Feedback Prize. The Feedback submission prize will be judged based on completeness, viability, and potential impact of the feedback. All Feedback Submissions must be completed by 11:59 PM PT on May 2, 2024.
