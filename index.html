The Free Hackathon

Hacknation is a massively collaborative hackathon where participants constantly share and debate ideas with the group to develop an innovative project. There are no teams, no prizes and no judges. Our existence stems from our experience that traditional hackathons often promote flashy but infeasible solutions, plagiarism and unhealthy working environments.

Join a worldwide hacker community building open-source projects.
Work on feasible project that can really make the world more beautiful, more fun, or just better
Learn by building with your peers.
An unbiased environment with realistic deadlines and a healthy mindset.
Be part of a collective intelligence for innovative projects, while having the time to do proper research.

Problem Statements

Problem statements take the form of introductions, offering a more in-depth explanation of the challenge. Each participant is free to design their own introductions and share them to collectively improve them, or to contribute directly to problems already proposed by other hackers.

build

Build solutions

During the remaining time, the community can use the previous introductions to develop solutions that can solve them. Again, each participant has the freedom to design their own solutions and share them to improve them collectively, or to contribute directly to solutions already proposed by other hackers. The same introduction may have different solutions.

At the end

By the end of the event, we should have several white papers, all of which we'll publish on our site. We'll rank them according to the number of hackers who have contributed to the white paper. All contributors will be cited, there will be no prizes or judging, and everything will be open source.
